VEGETABLE SALAD

Benefits of Vegetable Salad

Vegetable salads may be marinated or sauced mixtures of raw or cooked vegetables. They are commonly based on tomatoes, green beans, cucumbers, beets, and mushrooms. Cole slaw (from the Dutch kool, “cabbage”) is made of shredded or chopped cabbage with a mayonnaise or vinegar-based dressing.

Steps to make Vegetable Salad

  • Cut the tomatoes into 8 segments each lengthwise.
  • Mix the yogurt, salt, pepper, honey and vinegar and blend well together.
  • Keep both the vegetables and the dressing refrigerated separately until ready to serve.
  • >When ready to serve, mix the vegetables and the dressing together.
  • This has to be done at the last minute, as the vegetables combined with the salt tend to shed their juices and thus become limp and soggy if kept too long.
